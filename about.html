About Us

Marathwada Institute of Technology (MIT) Aurangabad

Marathwada Institute of Technology has been established in 1984 to cater the technical education in the rural part of Maharashtra. The institution spreads over 13 acres of land with separate hostel facilities for girls and boys. This Institute is approved by AICTE, New Delhi and is affiliated to Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar Technological University, Lonere, Raigarh, Maharashatra, the state technical university popularly known as BATU. The institution offers B. Tech. programmes in five disciplines and an Architecture program. Post-Graduation programs are also offered in Engineering & Technology, Computer Applications and Architecture. The Institute has a University recognized Ph.D. Research Center in Mechanical Engineering and Civil Engineering. More than 2500 full time & part time students are on campus for availing graduate and post graduate education and doing Ph.D. research.
